What we offer

Description of services

LoyalTrak is a software-as-a-service (SaaS) platform that helps local businesses retain customers and grow revenue through automated SMS text messaging. LoyalTrak is a product of Point5 LLC.

Our services include:

  • Missed call text-back: Automatically sends an SMS reply to anyone who calls your business and doesn't get an answer
  • SMS rewards program: A digital punch card system where customers text a keyword after each visit to earn stamps toward a reward
  • Referral program: Unique referral codes for each customer that track and reward word-of-mouth referrals automatically
  • Win-back messages: Automated SMS to customers who haven't visited in a set number of days (Growth and Pro plans)
  • Broadcast SMS: Send promotional text messages to your opted-in customer list (Growth and Pro plans)
  • Google review requests: Automated SMS asking satisfied customers to leave a Google review (Growth and Pro plans)
  • Business dashboard: A web-based portal to view customer stats, manage settings, and track activity

LoyalTrak is a digital service. No physical goods are sold or shipped. All features are delivered via web application and SMS text messaging.

Cancellation policy

Cancellation policy

Short version: Cancel any time from your dashboard or by emailing us. No cancellation fees. No notice period required. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period.

You may cancel your LoyalTrak subscription at any time, for any reason, with no penalty or cancellation fee.

How to cancel: Log in to your LoyalTrak dashboard at dashboard.loyaltrak.com → Settings → Account → Cancel subscription. Alternatively, email loyaltrak@outlook.com with "Cancel my account" in the subject line and we'll process it within one business day.

What happens when you cancel:

  • Your subscription will not renew at the next billing date
  • You retain full access to your dashboard and all features until the end of your current paid period
  • Your customer data remains available for export for 30 days after cancellation
  • All automated SMS messages (missed call replies, rewards, referrals) will stop at the end of your paid period
  • After 30 days, your data is permanently deleted from our systems in accordance with our privacy policy

Annual plan customers who cancel mid-year are subject to the refund policy below.

SMS & promotions

SMS messaging & promotional terms

LoyalTrak sends SMS text messages on behalf of businesses that use our platform. The following terms govern how those messages are sent and what recipients can expect.

Opt-in: End customers (the customers of businesses using LoyalTrak) opt in to SMS messages by texting a keyword to a business's designated number. This constitutes express written consent to receive SMS messages from that business via LoyalTrak. Customers can opt out at any time by texting STOP.

Message frequency: Message frequency varies by business. Customers may receive messages when they check in, earn a reward, receive a referral notification, or when the business sends a promotional broadcast. Standard message and data rates may apply.

Opt-out: Text STOP to any message to unsubscribe immediately. Text HELP for assistance. Once opted out, no further messages will be sent except a single confirmation of your opt-out.

Promotional broadcasts: Businesses on LoyalTrak Growth and Pro plans may send promotional SMS messages to their opted-in customer list. These messages are sent only to customers who have previously opted in by texting the business's keyword.

Terms of promotions: Any rewards, discounts, or offers sent via LoyalTrak are issued at the sole discretion of the individual business. LoyalTrak is not responsible for fulfillment of rewards or offers made by businesses using our platform. Contact the business directly for questions about a specific offer or reward.

Carrier compliance: LoyalTrak operates in compliance with US carrier regulations including A2P 10DLC registration requirements.

Privacy & data

Privacy & data handling

Business owner data: When you create a LoyalTrak account, we collect your name, email address, business name, and phone number. This information is used to operate your account, send you billing receipts, and provide customer support. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

End customer data: LoyalTrak stores phone numbers of customers who opt in to a business's SMS loyalty program. This data is stored on behalf of the business and is used solely to operate the loyalty program (tracking visits, sending rewards, processing referrals). End customer phone numbers are not shared with other businesses or sold to third parties.

Data export: Business owners can export their customer data at any time from the LoyalTrak dashboard.

Data deletion: Upon account cancellation, customer data is retained for 30 days to allow export, then permanently deleted. You may request immediate deletion by emailing loyaltrak@outlook.com.

Third-party services: LoyalTrak uses the following third-party services to operate the platform: Stripe (payment processing), Twilio (SMS delivery), and Airtable (data storage). Each of these services has its own privacy policy and data handling practices.
