What LoyalTrak does

All by text message.

Questions & answers

Plain answers to common questions

Does my customer need to download an app or create an account?
No — and that's the whole point. Your customers just send a text message. Nothing to download, no account to create, no password to remember. If they can send a text, they're in. This is why LoyalTrak works where loyalty apps often fail — there's no barrier to entry.
How long does setup take?
Most businesses are fully live in under 10 minutes. You create your account, enter your keyword and reward, and you're ready. We send you a test text right away so you can see it working before your first real customer uses it.
What happens when I miss a call?
Within about 30 seconds, LoyalTrak automatically sends an SMS to whoever called you. The message uses your business name and lets them know you saw their call. You don't have to do anything — it happens automatically every time, even when you're with another customer or closed for the day.
What if a customer tries to earn stamps without actually visiting?
LoyalTrak limits one stamp per calendar day per customer automatically — so texting multiple times in a single day only counts once. Pro plan customers can also enable a staff verification code, where the customer must include a 4-digit code that only your staff gives out after a real visit. This makes it completely fraud-proof for businesses with high-value rewards.
Which plan should I start on?
Most first-time customers start on Core — at $39/mo it's less than one missed appointment and includes all three core tools. Once you're seeing results and want to send promos or re-engage inactive customers, Growth is the natural next step. Pro makes sense when you open a second location or need airtight stamp verification for high-value rewards.
Is this legal? Do I need permission to text customers?
Yes, completely legal. When a customer texts your keyword, they initiate the conversation — that counts as consent under US SMS regulations. Every message also includes an opt-out option. Customers can text STOP at any time and LoyalTrak immediately stops messaging them. We handle all of this automatically.
Can I cancel if it's not working for me?
Absolutely. Cancel any time from your dashboard — no fees, no questions asked, no notice required. Your customer data always belongs to you and can be exported whenever you want.
